What is UvestFinance?

UvestFinance is a payment hub where merchants can accept payments globally and process online transactions. Basically, we help you make payment for goods and services via cryptocurrency and invest your revenue in assets.

How does UvestFinance works?

Uses can make payments using our cards on their different accounts or funded accounts like crypto and fx. Simply register your account and you can start making payments in crypto using our payment system.

Can I have more than one account on UvestFinance?

Yes, you have the option of registering one or more businesses on the UvestFinance platform.

Can I use UvestFinance cards for business purposes?

Yes, you can, provided its a regulated business(es).

What is a payment option?

Payment options are different options provided by UvestFinance that users can use in making online trnasactions.

Whos is qualified to use UvestFinance payment options?

Any registered and verified user can activate any of the payment options after passing the KYC (know your customer) verification.

How does UvestFinance validate transaction

All transactions carried out on our payment system are verified by our staffs and are logged for future references.

How do UvestFinance detect fraudulent transaction?

To process any funding transaction, a user must provide a proof of such transaction. If a user fails to provide such, the system will deny access to proceed.

How do i contact UvestFinance?

A user may contact UvestFinance support using either the contact page or emailing support@UvestFinance.co.uk for help.

Does UvestFinance provide instant support?

Yes. Using our on-site active support at the bottom-right corner.

Does UvestFinance accept cryptocurrency?

Yes. We have selective crypto currencies that we accept as means of transaction.

Is UvestFinance a crypto exchange platform?

No, we are not. We don't sell, buy, or trade crypto. We help our users process payments and invest their funds in assets.
